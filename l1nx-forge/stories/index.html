Story Bank

15 behavioral answers with collapsible chunks. Hide chunks to practice recall.

1.

Tell me about yourself.

~85s — Identity + Background + Thread
2.

Why this company / Why this role?

~72s — Company + Role + Fit
3.

Walk me through your current role and biggest impact.

~88s — STAR
4.

Tell me about a time you resolved a critical issue under pressure.

~85s — STAR + optional follow-up
5.

Describe a process improvement or automation you drove.

~66s — STAR
6.

Tell me about a time you showed initiative without being asked.

~62s — STAR
7.

How do you handle documentation, SOPs, and handoffs?

~82s — Process + Culture
8.

Describe a time you coordinated cross-functionally.

~65s — STAR
9.

What does your first 30 days look like?

~62s — Week-by-week
10.

Conflict or disagreement with a teammate.

~61s — STAR
11.

How do you prioritize a queue of 20+ tickets?

~56s — Method
12.

Tell me about a mistake and how you handled it.

~60s — STAR
13.

What excites you about a greenfield or high-growth environment?

~54s — Pattern match
14.

How do you handle on-call rotations, physical demands, and schedule flexibility?

~45s — Experience + Approach + Commitment
15.

Why should we hire you over other candidates?

~54s — Differentiator

Practice Notes

Drill daily: Q1, Q2, Q4, Q15 — opener, "why this role," pressure story, closer.
Drill 2x/week: Q3, Q5, Q6, Q13 — strongest differentiators (biggest impact, process wins, initiative, environment fit).
Drill 1x/week: Q7–Q12, Q14 — more formulaic, get the structure then let them flow.
Chunk practice: Deliver chunk [A] out loud until it flows. Chain A+B, then A+B+C. If any chunk takes >20s of hesitation to start, isolate and drill it. If any answer runs past 90s, cut the weakest sentence.
